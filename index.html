Peerless Tax Professionals

This site is for a new, tax prep service company located outside Columbus, OH. The owner was looking for an online presence that lives up to the company’s name, Peerless. I helped her automate several of her manual tasks and set the clear, supportive tone of the site. I also built a secure, client portal where clients can make payments, upload documents and check the status of their return. Under the hood, I installed Google Analytics and created Goals and Events to track conversions, button click and file downloads.

  • HTML5/CSS
  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • Calendly
  • Google Analytics
  • Let’s Encrypt SSL
  • Trello

Date Night Recipe

This app is a fun twist on ’Netflix & Chill’. The user starts by providing their basic info, including their zip code. Once they select their movie, we pass their zip to the Yelp API to find nearby restaurants with a cuisine that should pair well with the selected flick. We had a good time and took a lot of creative license determining the paired cuisine. For example, Fight Club returns nearby places that serve Meatloaf. Finally, users are presented with their movie and restaurant pairing as their “recipe” for a great date night.

  • HTML5/CSS
  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • Node/Express
  • Mongo/Mongoose
  • Mocha/Chai
  • Git/GitHub
  • Travis CI

Class Management App for Girl Develop It-Atlanta

As a GDI Class Organizer, we needed a simple, centralized way to manage the various resources (spreadsheets, Trello lists, HQ documents) needed to schedule and coordinate our training class offerings. We needed a way for everyone on the Leadership Committee to see, at a glance, our scheduled classes and the progress of each toward being fully “organized” (instructor, TAs, venue, Meetup.com event link). Once the class details are confirmed, the system pushes the details to Meetup.com via their API to create the new event, making it available to our members for registration.

  • React/Redux
  • CSS
  • Node/Express
  • Mongo/Mongoose
  • Enzyme/Jest
  • Git/GitHub
